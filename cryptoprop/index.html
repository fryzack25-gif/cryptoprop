FAQ

Is this real trading or a simulated evaluation?

All trading on CryptoProp is conducted in a simulated environment. No real assets are traded. Payouts are discretionary performance-based payments, not investment returns.

How do payouts work?

Once you pass both challenge steps and meet the profit buffer requirement, you become eligible for payouts. Profits are split 80% to the trader, 20% to CryptoProp. See the Payout Policy for full details.

What markets can I trade?

CryptoProp is spot-only. You can trade BTC, ETH, and major altcoin spot pairs. No perpetuals, no leverage products.

What happens if I breach a loss limit?

If you hit the trailing drawdown limit, your challenge fails. You can purchase a new attempt to start over from Step 1.
