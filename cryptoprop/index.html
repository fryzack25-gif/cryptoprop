FAQ

Is this real trading or a simulated evaluation?

All trading on CryptoProp is conducted in a simulated environment. No real assets are traded. Payouts are discretionary performance-based payments, not investment returns.

How do payouts work?

Define payout frequency, minimum thresholds, profit split, and any restrictions. Be clear about requirements (e.g., consistency rules).

What markets can I trade?

CryptoProp is spot-only. List supported spot pairs, supported exchanges, and any platform requirements.

What happens if I breach a loss limit?

Typically the account fails or is paused. State your reset policy and how traders can restart.
