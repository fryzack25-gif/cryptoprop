KYC / AML Policy

Effective Date: 2026  •  CryptoProp LLC  •  Indiana, United States

1. Purpose

This Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Policy describes the procedures used by CryptoProp LLC ("CryptoProp", "Company") to prevent fraud, financial crime, and misuse of the platform. The policy is designed to ensure that all users are properly identified and that the platform is not used for illegal activities.

2. Nature of the Platform

CryptoProp operates a simulated trading evaluation platform. No real trading accounts are provided and no user deposits are traded in financial markets. Users pay fees for access to simulated trading challenges designed to evaluate trading performance.

3. Identity Verification (KYC)

CryptoProp may require identity verification before allowing certain platform activities, including but not limited to:

  • Payout requests
  • Account upgrades
  • Suspicious account activity
  • Compliance checks

Users may be required to submit the following information:

  • Full legal name
  • Date of birth
  • Residential address
  • Government-issued photo identification
  • Selfie or biometric verification
  • Additional documents if requested

CryptoProp reserves the right to request additional documentation where necessary to verify identity.

4. Verification Process

Submitted identification documents may be reviewed using automated verification tools and manual review procedures. Accounts may be restricted until verification is successfully completed.

If identity verification cannot be completed or documentation appears fraudulent, CryptoProp may suspend or terminate the account.

5. AML Monitoring

CryptoProp monitors platform activity to detect suspicious behavior including:

  • Multiple account creation
  • Identity inconsistencies
  • Fraudulent payment activity
  • Attempts to circumvent platform rules
  • Coordinated activity between accounts

Accounts identified as high risk may be subject to enhanced review.

6. Suspicious Activity Review

If suspicious activity is detected, CryptoProp may:

  • Temporarily restrict account activity
  • Request additional verification documents
  • Delay or deny payouts
  • Permanently terminate the account
  • Report suspicious activity to appropriate authorities when required by law

7. Sanctions and Prohibited Jurisdictions

CryptoProp may restrict or prohibit access to users located in jurisdictions subject to international sanctions or regulatory restrictions. Users may not access the platform from regions where participation would violate applicable law.

8. Record Keeping

CryptoProp may retain KYC documentation and related records for compliance and security purposes. Records may be stored for a reasonable period consistent with fraud prevention and regulatory best practices.

9. Privacy and Data Protection

All personal information collected during the verification process is handled in accordance with the CryptoProp Privacy Policy. CryptoProp takes reasonable measures to protect user data and prevent unauthorized access.

10. Cooperation with Authorities

CryptoProp may cooperate with law enforcement or regulatory authorities where legally required, including the disclosure of information related to suspected financial crime.

11. Policy Updates

CryptoProp reserves the right to update this KYC and AML Policy at any time. Updated policies will be published on the CryptoProp website and may require user acknowledgment.

12. Contact

For questions regarding this policy or identity verification requirements, users may contact CryptoProp support through the official website.
