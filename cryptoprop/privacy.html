In certain situations, CryptoProp may request identity verification information to comply with fraud prevention procedures or regulatory requirements. Verification may include submission of identification documents and proof of address.

Failure to complete verification when requested may result in restricted access to platform features including payouts.

5. Payment Processing

Payments for platform services may be processed by third-party payment processors. CryptoProp does not store full payment card details. Payment information is handled by secure payment providers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Users may configure browser settings to refuse cookies; however, certain platform features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

CryptoProp takes reasonable measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. Security measures may include:

Information is only shared when necessary to operate the platform or comply with legal obligations.
